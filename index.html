Who We Are

Your One-Stop-Place for Industrial Solutions

ProTech Instruments UK Ltd is dedicated to empowering industries through precision measurement and monitoring, delivering accuracy, reliability, and sustainable performance.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver innovative, reliable, and sustainable measurement and monitoring solutions that empower industries to optimize processes, ensure compliance, and protect the environment. We are committed to providing advanced technologies that enhance water quality, process efficiency, air management, and equipment reliability for a safer, cleaner, and more sustainable future.

Our vision is to be a global leader in precision measurement and monitoring solutions, driving industrial excellence and environmental sustainability. We aim to set new standards in innovation, digital integration, and customer trust, helping industries worldwide achieve smarter operations and a more sustainable tomorrow.

Our Products

Precision Instruments & Solutions

A comprehensive portfolio of industrial measurement and monitoring products engineered for accuracy and reliability across every application.

Online Analyzers

Comprehensive water quality analytical systems for virtually every industry — ultrapure water, pure water, wastewater, process applications & SWAS (Steam and Water Analysis Systems). Parameters include:

  • Alkalinity
  • Ammonia & Ammonium
  • BOD (Biochemical Oxygen Demand)
  • Free Residual & Total Chlorine
  • COD (Chemical Oxygen Demand)
  • Conductivity
  • Dissolved Oxygen
  • Hardness (total, calcium & magnesium)
  • Hydrogen Sulfide
  • Nitrate & Total Nitrogen
  • ORP (Oxidation-Reduction Potential)
  • pH
  • Phosphorus
  • Silica & Sodium
  • TDS, TSS, TOC
  • Turbidity, toxicity, trace metals & more
Benchtop & Portable Lab Instruments

Laboratory instruments with pre-programmed methods for water analysis:

  • Spectrophotometers
  • Turbidimeters & Benchtop meters
  • Probes & Digital reactors
  • Titration systems
  • TOC analyzers

Portable field instruments:

  • Pocket colorimeters
  • Portable parallel analyzers (PPA)
  • Fluorometers & more
Controllers & Transmitters

Universal digital, real-time transmitters offering flexible connectivity options tailored to your operation. Remote access capabilities ensure convenient data monitoring and simplified maintenance — keeping your processes optimised at all times.

Pressure Transmitters

Accurate and stable pressure measurement — critical for safe, reliable, and profitable plant operations.

  • Gauge Pressure
  • Differential Pressure
  • Absolute Pressure
  • Draft Pressure
  • Mass Flow (Multivariable)
  • Low Flow
Temperature Transmitters

A complete range of temperature transmitters in multiple configurations to suit diverse application needs:

  • Field Mount
  • Head Mount
  • Panel Mount
Flow Meters

High-quality flow measurement solutions delivering accuracy, reliability, and performance for diverse industrial applications:

  • Coriolis Mass Flowmeters
  • Electro-magnetic Flowmeters
  • Vortex Flowmeters
  • Variable Area Flowmeters (Rotameters)
  • Ultrasonic Flowmeters
  • Flow Controllers
Level Meters

Comprehensive level measurement solutions designed for accuracy and reliability across industrial applications:

  • Guided Wave Radar Level Transmitters
  • Non-contact Radar Level Transmitters
  • Ultrasonic Level Transmitters
  • Level Indicators
Gauges & Switches

A range of gauges combining accuracy, simplicity, and dependable performance — even in harsh operating conditions — ensuring precise monitoring across all your processes.

Our complete range includes:

  • Pressure gauges
  • Temperature gauges
  • Level gauges
  • Gauge accessories
  • Temperature elements — Thermocouples and RTDs
  • Pressure switches
  • Temperature switches
  • Level switches
  • Flow switches
